**Name:** wgm

**Description:**

```text

```

#### &gt; Whisper to Token

**Name:** wch

**Description:**

```text
/emas @{selected|token_name} speaks low.
/w @{target|token_name} ?{"What"|um}
```

#### &gt; Miscellaneous D20 Rol

**Name:** roll

**Description:**

```text
/me rolls a [[1d20+?{modifier|0}]] for ?{reason|no reason}.
```

#### &gt; Calculator

**Name:** calc

**Description:**

```text
**=** [[?{Calculator}]]
```

#### Turn /talktomyself on/off

**Name:** ttms

**Description:**

```text

```

#### Post an Image

**Name:** img

**Description:**

```text

```
